Guided Jeep Tours

Our guided jeep tours will show you:

● numerous wildflowers and rare plants

● a large variety wildlife

● high-alpine meadows

● avalanche gullies

● Impressive waterfalls and geological features

Follow the La Plata River to La Plata City, an old mining town established in 1875. Continue to the peak of Kennebec Pass at 12,000 feet above sea level.

See all the way to Cortez and Telluride from the top of the pass!

Kids are extra entertained with our “Pan For Gold” scavenger hunt. Find the items on the checklist and get some fool’s gold!

The roads are not well maintained and not often used which results in a bumpy, bouncy tour! Bring sun cover, suncream, a reusable water bottle, snacks, and your camera!

Adventure Level - Mild

Location - Durango. In the Mountains between Durango and Silverton

Family Friendly - Yes

Season - May to Early October

Numbers - Single up to Groups of 8 people
