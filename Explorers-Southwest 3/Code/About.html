Based in Durango, Colorado, USA, Explorers Southwest is a three season (March to October) “mild to mildly adventurous” tours company. Our flagship and most popular offer — which started our business off in 2008 — was guided jeep tours to local “ghost mining towns” in the mountains between the towns of Durango and Silverton. We also offer a fully guided tour to Mesa Verde National Park one hour away.
